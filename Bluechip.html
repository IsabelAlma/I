Blue Chip Scholars

Background

Through the fantastic Hexagon UX Houston, I was able to participate in a three-part design sprint series to help create a solution for Houston non-profit Blue Chip Scholars (BCS). BCS is a local organization that connects young women interested in STEM with mentors in STEM careers. Our goal was to create design for an app that can be used by both mentors and mentees to communicate progress, achievements, and feedback in the STEM related projects the mentees chose to work on.

Role

Uxer in Training

Methods & Tools

  • Empathy Mapping
  • Low Fidelity Wireframing
  • High Fidelity Wireframing
  • Mockups
  • Prototype
  • Usability Testing

Outcome

Over the course of three months, May 2020 to June 2020, the team was able to design and test our collaborated app idea. Methods from empathy mapping, storyboarding, to a working prototype that we tested with the mentors and mentees were used. This was an excellent learning opportunity that granted me a great deal of insight in the UX process.
